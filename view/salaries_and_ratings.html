What is the conclusion 💻?

The conclusion is that it is encouraged for everyone to fact check the data themselves. Even though the data shows a correlation between rating and salary, it's important to remember that correlation does not imply causation.

An example of this is: in the College of Nursing, the professors are full-time nurses. This means that some of them are part-time professors, so they won't get paid as much as full-time professors.

Things to mention are that the data is not recently updated, as some professors are not listed in the data. Also, professor roles matter here as well, so it's important to take them into account when looking at the salary.

We can't blindly believe what we see on the web.
This was made to encourage people to make sense out of data themselves and find out why things are the way they are, taking into account many factors.
