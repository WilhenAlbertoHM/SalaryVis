Something that stood out from these graphs is Amherst's graph for coaches. There was a spike in the salaries of coaches between 2020 and 2022, which was when the COVID-19 pandemic was happening. In 2020, they hired Don Brown as their new head coach, which could explain the spike in salaries. However, we see a higher spike in 2022, as they hired Frank Martin as their new head coach.

These two coaches are well known in the sports world, and having them lead the young players at UMass Amherst in the NCAA Division I level is a privilege.

However, it's important to recognize that faculty members are also important in the university setting. They are the ones who teach the students and help them grow academically. Not everyone is involved in sports, so it's important to not forget about faculty members.

This was done with the purpose of visualizing data and see what we can learn from data. If you'd like to get more information about the data, data is available upon request.
